Magic mods involve using supernatural powers to alter the very world of minecraft, or enchant, infuse, transmute items, blocks into others.

Astral Sorcery

This mod involves using the power of the stars to create different items. It features different altars which often involve multi-block structures made of Marble, the main block of this mod, which can be utilized to craft a variety of astral-y items, infuse items with starlight resulting in newer, better items and many more. A common occurence in this mod is the necessity of multi-block structures without which the altars would not function. Also, as would be expected, the starry sky can be observed via the use of a handheld telescope, a placed one or in the late game, an observator.

    Pros:
  • a really fun to use mod
  • very appealing graphics, an abundence of animations, especially when crafting
    Cons:
  • almost endless amount of multi-block structures which can often be costly and timely to create

Botania

This mod involves using the power of the... plants to do your bidding, although mana doesn't allow for a lot of possibilities- they mostly consist in the making of runes, which are utilized in crafting recipes that mostly consist of either pointless stuff, or equipment, and also in the transmutation of items, both via the mana pool and the Alfheimian portal. The end game is mostly just the boss fight against the Guardian of Gaia, the loot of which is also usable only in a small amount of recipes, all being equipment.

    Pros:
  • simple to learn & easy to apply in the in-game, the Lexica Botania is suitable enough for a tutorial
    Cons:
  • lacking in content
  • basically useless when integrating other mods
