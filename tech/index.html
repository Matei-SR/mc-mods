Tech mods - short for mods revolving around technology are ones that are based on machines that automate certain aspects of the game. They usually involve cable management, multi-block machines and different types of armor, usually granting flight via Jetpacks.

Thermal Expansion

This mod revolves around a large list of machines with different functions, including a Pulverizer, which you can use to grind ores into x2 dust, which can then be smelt into 1 ingot each, and also an array of Dynamos which take some kind of fuel(be it smeltables, liquid or coins) and use it to generate RF.

Ender IO

This mod relies around a small array of machines that are similar to the TE ones, although they work at a faster rate, of 3 items per smelt/pulverize, and sometimes they give extra loot. The main difference is that EIO also features more machines that improve automation, including an autofarmer, faster conduits that are configurable with speed up/downgrades, filters, inputs/outputs, redstone sensitivity, and most importantly, channels. The base of all apparatuses is Grains of Infinity, which are obtained by burning bedrock as it appears into every single chassis.

Applied Energistics 2

One of the most well-known tech mods of all time- this one is an old-timer. It's really hard to grasp- but once you finally understand the ins and outs you'll be able to create very overpowered, yet complicated and awful on your eyes systems. Basically, this mod relies on one thing: ME. What is ME you may ask? Every single item/fluid/gas can be stored as simple 'disk space' on cards, which can then be used with ME Chests, which can only store 1 disk, or Disk Drives which have to be part of an ME system, which also has to include a controller, a (crafting) grid, and probably cables. This mod also allows you to heavily automate item distribution and crafting, enabling you to build custom sized multi-blocks with the purpose of inputting item into furnaces, or other mods' machines, and then outputting them back into the ME System (in a disk most likely).
